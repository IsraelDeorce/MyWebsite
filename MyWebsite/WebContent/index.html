A bit about me

I was born in the city of Vitoria-ES,Brazil and moved to Porto Alegre in 2016 to study Software Engineering at the Faculty of Informatics of the PUCRS. I worked as a monitor student of the Introduction to Software Engineering Course and I also participated actively in the projects of the Experimental Software Engineering Agency (AGES) where I had the opportunity to work developing many software solutions

Traveling and learning new languages are among my favorite Hobbies. I achieved a diploma Advanced English in a six months ESL course that I took in Vancouver-BC Canada. Nowadays I've been performing a Scholarship work as a Undergraduate Researcher for the PUCRS in parallel with my major studies and other personal projects.

Sharing Platform to Generate Audiobooks

This JavaWeb project of the AGES helps people with visual disabilities, dyslexia or even more audio oriented users by providing a platform that helps the creation of Audio Books. The system gives the publisher the possibility to registrar Books, Chapters and Segments in PDF format and to managed them. These Segments will be available to download by audio donators who will submit their audio helping to form the Audio Books.

  • Client: ediPUCRS
  • Date: August 2016
  • Service: Java Web Development

More Interaction in the University

A Project that targets Universities audiences that aims to increase and facilitate the integration and interaction among the students themselves and the students with the University. The system consists in a social network focused on giving tools to the students of the PUCRS to reach their main social demands. Among these tools, we can list the management of book exchanging between the students, the creation of groups of similar interests and the easy access to information of events of the University, increasing significantly the many possibilities for the student in the University and enriching the experience on campus since the students will have more interaction with the academia.

  • Client: Grupo do Baralho
  • Date: 2016
  • Service: Java Web Development
